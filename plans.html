Campus Reader: The Educational Social Network

This is a social network (currently planned for GNDU only).
Honestly, the messy and unorganised education system makes it really hard for those who wish to learn in the university.
We are given the complete subject notes just days before the exam. Moreover, It has become the sole responsibility of the CR’s to share those in the whatsapp groups which is an inefficient way to learn things on a regular basis.
Thus, the project that I’m currently working upon is based on the simple idea of being able to share the study material and keep an organised record of it. Also, interactions among different catagories of the people in the department will make it much more reliable than checking whatapp groups for updates by the HOD, Student Co-ordinator and CRs. The project is aimed to be a combination of facebook, quora and piazza.
It will initially target the CSE Deptt in the university only. Then if it proves to be efficient, any deptt can use it for academic and networking purposes.
The project has been started in jsp, servlets, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, ajax.
